  • Showcase video

    • I would say one of the best shaders out of this project was the shadow enemy (made with Amplify Shader Editor), Initially i was asked to do a "liquid" type of movement for a slash this was to reflect "Umbra" as is called in the game, so i gather reference and ended getting the one on the left of the video. Here you can see nodes on how its made, essentially scrolling two noises between each other within the UVs of the mesh (can be made with one with multiples channels). It also includes a outline, but for that you only extrude normals with a color in a extra pass.

    After that, the shader had a natural evolution for more flexibility over the noise channels and a mask for so you can decide wehere you want the effect (the one on the right) Few more design choices over transparency and colors so it ended up being like the one in the middle of the video.
